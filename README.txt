Group project frequently cause stress for students due to difficulties with tasks delegation, communication and progress tracking, existing methods such as email threads and messenger apps are unorganized and unsuitable for academic team work, resulting in inconsistent workloads, mixed deadlines and irritation, A poll indicated that 75% of students find group project stressful with half attributing this to poor coordination. This project seeks to provide a web-based application specifically tailored for students to facilitate communication, by combining task management and progress tracking into a single, user-friendly platform, the solution enables students to focus on their work without burden.
